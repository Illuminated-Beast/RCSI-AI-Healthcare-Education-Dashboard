449 of 507 included papers were published after November 2022, when general-purpose generative AI chat tools reached mainstream use. The field did not gradually adopt AI - it accelerated almost entirely within a 2-year window (2023–2025). This is not a decade-long trend; it is a step-change, and the analysis is structured around it.

Integrated Findings - Cross-cutting Insights
Finding 1

The Field is Post-Inflection by Overwhelming Margin

88.6% of papers were published after November 2022. This is not a decade of gradual adoption - it is a 2-year sprint. Any analysis of "the last decade" is structurally dominated by 2023–2025.

Finding 2

Headline D1/D2 Inversion - Benchmarking & Exam Performance

94.0% outcome reporting (highest in corpus) against only 7.8% rigorous design (second-lowest). The field measures exam accuracy constantly but almost never with a controlled or randomised design. Replicated across 3 BERTopic re-fits.

Finding 3

Reverse Inversion - Imaging & Surgical Education

Best design quality in the corpus (29.9% rigorous, HIGH) but only 50.5% outcome reporting (LOW). Rigorous studies exist here - they just don't report a measurable result.

Finding 4

Third Inversion - Perceptions, Readiness & Knowledge-Attitude

82.1% outcome reporting but only 3.6% rigorous design - the lowest D2 in the entire corpus. Attitude surveys report a number reliably; they just don't test anything rigorously.

Finding 5

Discipline Classification Has a Structural Blind Spot

62.9% of papers (319/507) fall into "Cross-disciplinary / Not specified" under the Stage 5 keyword classifier. This reflects a keyword-matching limitation, not necessarily a genuinely undifferentiated literature - flag this as a methods caveat, not a headline result.

Finding 6

Nursing & Allied Health are Emerging, Not Declining

Nursing (18) and Allied Health (2) together are 3.9% of the corpus, with zero papers before 2023. Frame as an emerging niche with growth potential, not an established gap.

Research Pipeline - Stages 0 to 7
STAGE 0

Data Preparation

Filtered initial records to 3 target categories, deduplicated to 507 screening and 506 combined-results records.

STAGE 1

Corpus Construction

Normalised classification variants to 3 canonical categories. Extracted years from mixed date formats. Added outcome-reported regex flag.

STAGE 2

BERTopic NLP Modelling (Locked)

BERTopic with KMeans backend, 507 documents, 6 topics. Model saved to disk - never re-run. Topic assignment by exact label match only.

STAGE 3

Statistical Analysis

Chi-square tests with Cramér's V effect sizes. Status: requires local re-verification against the locked Stage 2 export before these are treated as final.

STAGE 5

Gap Identification

Regex-based study-design and discipline classification. Keyword-based - see caveats on the Evidence Gap tab.

STAGE 6

Evidence Scoring

D1 (outcome reporting) × 0.5 + D2 (rigorous design) × 0.3 + D3 (vocabulary maturity) × 0.2. Locked constants, never recomputed from raw data.
